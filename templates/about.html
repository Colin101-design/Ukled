About Us

Established in 2012, UKLED has become one of the leading manufacturers of high-quality LED lighting solutions. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility enables us to produce an extensive range of LED products, including bulbs, panels, and strip lights, all designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Over the years, we have partnered with various large-scale clients such as Pizza Express and Laura Ashley, providing energy-efficient lighting solutions for commercial spaces, restaurants, and retail outlets. Even though some of our earlier clients have moved on, our commitment to innovation and excellence remains stronger than ever.

Our focus is not just on manufacturing but also on research and development. With a dedicated team of engineers and technicians, we continuously improve our products, ensuring they are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and environmentally friendly. From product design to final assembly, we maintain strict quality control standards to ensure each LED light meets global certification requirements.

At UKLED, sustainability is a core value. We aim to reduce our carbon footprint by using eco-friendly materials and processes. Our LED lights are built to last, reducing waste and energy consumption while providing superior illumination. Whether you need customized lighting for large-scale projects or standard products for everyday use, UKLED is your trusted partner.

Our journey in LED technology is fueled by a passion for lighting innovation, ensuring that we continue to lead the industry with cutting-edge products and unmatched customer service. We look forward to illuminating your future!
