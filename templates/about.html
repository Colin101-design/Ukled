About Us

Established in 2012, UKLED has become one of the leading manufacturers of high-quality LED lighting solutions. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility enables us to produce an extensive range of LED products, including bulbs, panels, and strip lights, all designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Over the years, we have partnered with various large-scale clients such as Pizza Express and Laura Ashley, providing energy-efficient lighting solutions for commercial spaces, restaurants, and retail outlets. Even though some of our earlier clients have moved on, our commitment to innovation and excellence remains stronger than ever.
